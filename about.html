My Favorite Activity: Helping Patients

I chose this as my favorite activity because it felt good to help people when they were stressed. It showed me that being a professional isn't just about technical skills; it is also about helping others feel safe and understood.

Activity Reflection: Healthcare Communication (STAR)

In December 2025, I volunteered to help patients who did not speak English. I used translation services to help them talk to their doctors. The main problem was that these patients could not understand important medical information. My goal was to make sure they got the right help and felt comfortable. I worked closely with the translators and stayed very patient. I made sure to listen to the patients and tell the doctors exactly what they said. This activity helped me grow because I learned how to talk to people from different cultures. It also taught me how to stay professional in a busy environment. These skills are very important for my future career in business and finance.
